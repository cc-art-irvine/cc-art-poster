课程政策 Course Policy

请仔细阅读以下政策条款 Please read the following policy terms carefully

📝 报名政策 Registration Policy

试课安排 Trial Class Arrangement

  • • 首次孩子可参加 $25试课，体验课程内容和教学方式• New students may attend a $25 trial class to experience the course content and teaching style
  • • 试课包含所有材料费用，无额外收费• The trial class includes all material fees, no extra charges
  • • 试课后可决定是否购买正式课程套餐• After the trial, you may decide whether to purchase a regular course package
  • • 试课费用不可抵扣正式课程费用• The trial class fee cannot be deducted from regular course fees

年龄要求 Age Requirements

  • • 建议年龄：3岁及以上• Recommended age: 3 years and above
  • • 3-5岁儿童需家长陪同• Children aged 3-5 must be accompanied by a parent
  • • 成人课程欢迎零基础孩子• Adult classes welcome beginners
  • • 特殊需求孩子请提前沟通• Please communicate any special needs in advance

课包规则 Package Rules

  • • 课包购买后有效期为12个月• Packages are valid for 12 months after purchase
  • • 课包不可转让给他人• Packages are non-transferable
  • • 课包余额不可提现• Remaining package balance is non-refundable as cash
  • • 过期课包恕不延期• Expired packages cannot be extended

📅 预约与取消政策 Booking & Cancellation Policy

课程预约 Class Booking

  • • 需至少提前24小时预约课程• Classes must be booked at least 24 hours in advance
  • • 可通过微信、电话或网站预约• Booking available via WeChat, phone, or website
  • • 预约成功后会收到确认信息• Confirmation will be sent after successful booking
  • • 建议定期预约以确保上课连续性• Regular booking is recommended for continuous learning

取消课程 Class Cancellation

  • • 需至少提前24小时取消课程• Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance
  • • 24小时内取消将扣除一次课时• Cancellations within 24 hours will result in one class being deducted
  • • 无故缺席将扣除一次课时• No-shows will result in one class being deducted
  • • 紧急情况可特殊处理• Emergencies may be handled on a case-by-case basis

改期规则 Rescheduling Policy

  • • 可免费改期一次（需提前24小时）• One free reschedule allowed (must be requested 24 hours in advance)
  • • 同一课程多次改期需支付$10手续费• Multiple reschedules for the same class incur a $10 fee each
  • • 改期课程需在原课程时间后30天内完成• Rescheduled classes must be completed within 30 days of the original date
  • • 特殊情况下可协商处理• Special cases may be negotiated

🌤️ 天气政策 Weather Policy

恶劣天气 Severe Weather

  • • 雨天、雪天或极端天气将自动改期• Classes will be rescheduled in case of rain, snow, or extreme weather
  • • 老师会提前2-4小时通知孩子• Notification will be sent 2-4 hours in advance
  • • 可免费改期至合适时间• Free rescheduling to a suitable time
  • • 如孩子坚持上课，将安排室内替代课程• If students wish to continue, an indoor alternative will be arranged

微雨天气 Light Rain

  • • 小雨天气下可选择继续上课（有遮蔽处）• In light rain, classes may continue in sheltered areas
  • • 孩子可选择改期，不收取任何费用• Students may choose to reschedule at no extra cost
  • • 课程会根据天气调整内容• Class content may be adjusted according to weather
  • • 安全第一，最终决定权在老师• Safety first; the teacher has final authority

🛡️ 安全政策 Safety Policy

孩子安全 Student Safety

  • • 户外课程选址确保安全性• Outdoor class locations are selected for safety
  • • 课程区域禁止无关人员进入• Unrelated persons are not allowed in the class area
  • • 建议购买个人意外保险• Personal accident insurance is recommended

健康要求 Health Requirements

  • • 孩子身体不适时请勿上课• Do not attend class if feeling unwell
  • • 有传染性疾病时需居家休息• Stay home if you have a contagious illness
  • • 特殊病史需提前告知老师• Inform the teacher in advance of any special medical history
  • • 过敏体质请提前说明• Please inform in advance if you have allergies

材料安全 Material Safety

  • • 使用无毒环保颜料• Only non-toxic, eco-friendly paints are used
  • • 画笔等工具定期消毒• Brushes and tools are regularly sanitized
  • • 禁止将材料放入口中• Do not put materials in your mouth
  • • 课后需清洁双手• Wash hands after class

💰 退费政策 Refund Policy

试课退费 Trial Class Refund

  • • 试课费用概不退还• Trial class fees are non-refundable
  • • 如因老师原因无法上课，可全额退款• Full refund if the class is cancelled by the teacher
  • • 如因天气原因，可免费改期或退款• Free reschedule or refund in case of weather cancellation

课包退费 Package Refund

  • • 购买后48小时内，未使用课时可申请退款 80%• Within 48 hours of purchase, 80% refund for unused classes
  • • 购买后48小时后，未使用课时可申请退款 20%• After 48 hours, 20% refund for unused classes
  • • 购买后14天后，不与退款• No refunds after 14 days of purchase
  • • 已使用课时按单节课价格计算• Used classes are charged at the single class rate
  • • 课程一旦开始使用（无论剩余课时多少）不可转课退款。• Once a package is started (regardless of remaining classes), no transfer or refund is allowed
  • • 退款金额 = 课包总价 - 已使用课时×单节价• Refund amount = package price - (used classes × single class price)
  • • 退款将在7-14个工作日内处理• Refunds will be processed within 7-14 business days

特殊情况 Special Circumstances

  • • 搬家或长期出差可协商处理• Moving or long-term business trips may be negotiated
  • • 重大疾病或意外可特殊考虑• Major illness or accident may be specially considered
