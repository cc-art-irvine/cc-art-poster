💡 教学理念 Teaching Philosophy

艺术不仅仅是技法的传授，更是创造力和想象力的启发。我相信每个孩子都有独特的艺术天赋，需要的是耐心的引导和专业的指导。Art is not just about technique, but about inspiring creativity and imagination. I believe every child has unique artistic talent that needs patient guidance and professional instruction.

通过户外写生，让孩子在大自然中感受色彩的变化、光影的魅力，培养敏锐的观察力和对美的感知能力。Through outdoor sketching, students experience the changes of color and the charm of light and shadow in nature, cultivating keen observation and a sense of beauty.

结合我在3D艺术和传统绘画方面的专业背景，为孩子提供多元化的艺术体验，开拓更广阔的创作视野。Combining my background in 3D art and traditional painting, I offer students a diverse artistic experience and a broader creative vision.
